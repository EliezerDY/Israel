David's Tower

A citadel located near the Jaffe Gate entrance to the Old City of Jerusalem. The Byzantienes believed it to be the palace of King David and dubbed it Tower of David. It is believed that in actuality it was built by King Herod. There is a museum located in it which describes the history of the Old City. At night there is a light show which describes the history of King David.
